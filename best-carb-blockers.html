Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do carb blockers actually work?
Yes, research shows 20-30% carb absorption reduction. Most effective with high-carb meals (>50g carbs).
Q: What side effects do carb blockers have?
Initial digestive bloating and loose stools as your gut adjusts. Usually subsides after 5-10 uses.
Q: Best carb blocker for weight loss?
White kidney bean extract (Phase 2) has the most clinical trials. Combine with calorie deficit for best results.
Q: Can I take carb blockers daily?
Yes, they're safe for daily use. Take before your largest carb meal of the day.
Q: Does carb blocker work on fiber?
No, they only block digestible carbs and simple starches. Fiber is unaffected.
