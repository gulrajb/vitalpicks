Why D3+K2 Together?

Vitamin D3 improves calcium absorption — but without K2, that calcium can deposit in arteries instead of bones. The D3+K2 combination directs calcium where it belongs. Always buy D3 (cholecalciferol), not D2 — it's far more effective.

💡 How Much Vitamin D Do You Need?

General maintenance

1,000–2,000 IU/day is safe for most adults maintaining adequate levels. If you're deficient (confirmed by blood test), 4,000–5,000 IU/day for 3 months is commonly recommended.

Get tested first

A 25(OH)D blood test costs very little. Optimal range is 40–60 ng/mL. This tells you exactly how much to supplement rather than guessing.
