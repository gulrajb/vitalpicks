⚡ What Makes a Good Pre-Workout?

The best pre-workouts have three things: clinically dosed caffeine (150–200mg), beta-alanine for endurance, and citrulline malate for pump. Avoid anything that hides ingredients behind "proprietary blends" — that's a red flag for underdosing.

🧠 Pre-Workout Buying Guide

How much caffeine do you need?

Beginners: 100–175mg. Intermediate: 200–300mg. Advanced: 300mg+. Never exceed 400mg per day total (including coffee).

What ingredients actually matter?

Must-haves: Caffeine, L-Citrulline (6g+), Beta-Alanine. Nice to have: L-Theanine (smooths caffeine), Betaine Anhydrous. Ignore: Any "proprietary blend" that doesn't show exact doses.

When to take it

20–30 minutes before your workout. Don't take it within 6 hours of bedtime or you'll wreck your sleep.
