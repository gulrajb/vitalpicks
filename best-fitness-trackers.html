🧠 How to Choose the Right Fitness Tracker

For Beginners

Start with the Mi Band 8. It's cheap, easy to use, and tracks everything you need. If you love it after 3 months, upgrade to something more premium.

For Serious Fitness Enthusiasts

Go with the Fitbit Charge 6. Built-in GPS means you don't need your phone on runs, and the health insights are genuinely useful for training.

For iPhone Users

Apple Watch SE is worth every rupee/dollar if you're in the Apple ecosystem. The health and safety features alone justify the price.

🏆 Our Final Verdict

For most people, the Fitbit Charge 6 offers the best balance of features and price. Budget shoppers should grab the Mi Band 8 without hesitation. iPhone users — just get the Apple Watch SE and don't look back.
