FAQs

Do I need GPS on my smartwatch?

Yes, for running, cycling, and outdoor activities. GPS tracks your route, distance, and pace accurately. Without it, your watch estimates distance using arm motion, which is less accurate.

What's the difference between a smartwatch and a fitness tracker?

Smartwatches have full operating systems with apps, notifications, and smart features. Fitness trackers are simpler, focusing on activity, heart rate, and sleep tracking.

Which smartwatch has the longest battery life?

Garmin watches lead with 11-14 days. Apple Watch gets 4-6 days. Samsung Galaxy gets 3-4 days.

Can I swim with these smartwatches?

Yes, most are water-resistant to 50m depth, which covers swimming, snorkeling, and shower use. Not for diving.
