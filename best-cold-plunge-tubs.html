Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How often should I use a cold plunge?

3-4 times per week is ideal for recovery benefits without overtraining.

❓ What temperature is best?

50-59°F (10-15°C) is optimal for recovery benefits.

❓ How long should I stay in?

Start with 1-2 minutes and work up to 3-5 minutes as you adapt.
