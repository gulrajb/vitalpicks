🧠 How to Choose the Right Protein Powder

With so many options, here's a simple framework to pick the right one for you:

1. Know Your Goal

Muscle gain: Go for whey concentrate or isolate with 24g+ protein per serving. ON Gold Standard or MuscleBlaze are perfect.

Weight loss: Look for low-carb options under 5g carbs per serving. Whey isolate is your best friend.

Everyday health: A simple whey concentrate like MyProtein is more than enough.

2. Check the Label

Always check the protein per 100g — it should be above 70g. Anything lower means you're paying for fillers. Also check for added sugars and artificial ingredients if those matter to you.

3. Consider Your Budget

You don't need to spend a lot to get good protein. MyProtein gives you 80%+ of the benefit at half the price of premium brands. But if you can afford ON Gold Standard, the taste and quality difference is noticeable.
