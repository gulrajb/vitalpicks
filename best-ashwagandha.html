💡 How to Take Ashwagandha

Dosage

300–600mg of a standardized extract (KSM-66 or Sensoril) daily. Split into two doses (morning and evening) for best results.

How long until it works?

Most people notice better sleep within 1–2 weeks. Stress reduction and strength improvements take 4–8 weeks of consistent use.

Should you cycle it?

Most experts suggest taking it for 3 months, then a 2-week break. There's no strong evidence you must cycle, but it's a sensible precaution with adaptogens.
