🔬 Why Omega-3 Matters

Most people are severely deficient in omega-3s because modern diets are loaded with omega-6 (vegetable oils, processed food) and low in oily fish. This imbalance drives chronic inflammation. Supplementing with 1–3g of combined EPA+DHA daily is one of the highest-impact health habits you can build.

💡 How Much Omega-3 Do You Need?

Daily dosage

Aim for 1,000–2,000mg of combined EPA+DHA per day for general health. For inflammation or cardiovascular concerns, studies support up to 3,000mg/day. Always look at EPA+DHA on the label — not total fish oil (which includes other fats).

When to take it

Take fish oil with a meal that contains fat — this significantly improves absorption. Morning or evening doesn't matter, just be consistent.

Signs your fish oil is oxidized (bad)

If it smells strongly fishy when you open the bottle, or you get strong fishy burps — it's oxidized and providing little benefit. Fresh fish oil should smell mildly oceanic, not rancid.
