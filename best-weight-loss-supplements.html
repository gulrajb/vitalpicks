🧠 What Actually Causes Fat Loss

The only thing that works: calorie deficit

You lose fat when you consume fewer calories than you burn. Period. Supplements can make this slightly easier — by boosting energy, reducing appetite, or marginally increasing metabolism — but they cannot override a calorie surplus.

Protein is your best weight loss tool

High protein intake preserves muscle during a cut, keeps you full longer, and requires more energy to digest than carbs or fat. Before buying any fat burner, make sure your protein intake is high enough — aim for 0.8–1g per pound of bodyweight.

Realistic expectations

The best supplements in this category provide a 5–10% boost to your results. If you're losing 1 lb per week through diet and exercise, supplements might help you lose 1.05–1.1 lbs. That's real — but not magic.
