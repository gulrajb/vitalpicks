Buying Guide

Blood glucose monitors fall into two categories: 1) **Finger-stick meters** - traditional, affordable, require small blood samples, instant results, 2) **Continuous Glucose Monitors (CGMs)** - wearable sensors, no finger pricks, real-time trends, higher cost. Choose based on: 1) **Testing frequency** - if multiple daily tests, CGM is more convenient, 2) **Insurance coverage** - Medicare/insurance often covers CGMs for insulin users, 3) **Integration** - smartphone connectivity for trend analysis, 4) **Accuracy** - all FDA-cleared devices meet standards, 5) **Lifestyle** - CGMs allow water exposure and active monitoring. Combo approach: use meters for spot-checks with CGM for daily management.
