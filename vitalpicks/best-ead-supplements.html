Why Choose EAA Supplements?

Essential Amino Acids (EAAs) are the building blocks your muscles need to grow and recover. Unlike BCAAs, EAAs contain all 9 essential amino acids your body cannot produce on its own. They're scientifically proven to boost muscle protein synthesis, reduce fatigue, and accelerate recovery—making them perfect for serious athletes and fitness enthusiasts.

💡 Buying Guide: How to Choose the Best EAA

1. Check the label: Look for all 9 essential amino acids: Histidine, Isoleucine, Leucine, Lysine, Methionine, Phenylalanine, Threonine, Tryptophan, and Valine.

2. Dosage per serving: A good EAA supplement should contain 5-10g of total EAAs per serving.

3. Ingredient quality: Prefer natural ingredients or at least transparent labeling of what goes into each scoop.

4. Taste & mixability: You'll be drinking this regularly, so flavor and mixability matter.

5. Price per serving: Quality EAA supplements typically cost $1-2 per serving. Don't overpay for brand names.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What's the difference between EAAs and BCAAs?
EAAs contain all 9 essential amino acids, while BCAAs only contain 3 (branched-chain). EAAs are more comprehensive for muscle growth and recovery.
❓ When should I take EAA supplements?
Best taken during or immediately after workouts for maximum absorption and muscle protein synthesis. You can also use them between meals for extra amino acid intake.
❓ Are EAA supplements safe?
Yes, EAAs are naturally occurring amino acids found in food. They're generally safe for healthy adults when taken as directed. Always consult your doctor if you have health concerns.
❓ Can I use EAAs while cutting?
Absolutely! EAAs are excellent during a cut because they preserve muscle mass while you're in a calorie deficit. They contain minimal calories too.
❓ Do EAAs taste good?
Modern EAA supplements come in many flavors and taste quite good. Popular brands offer 20+ flavor options, so you'll find something you like.
