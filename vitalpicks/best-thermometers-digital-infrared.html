Buying Guide

When choosing a digital thermometer, consider: 1) **Measurement method** - oral, under-arm, forehead, or ear depending on age and preference, 2) **Speed** - faster readings (under 10 seconds) for fussy children, 3) **Accuracy** - FDA clearance confirms clinical accuracy, 4) **Features** - memory function, auto-shutoff, fever alerts, 5) **Waterproofing** - essential for easy cleaning, 6) **Display** - backlit screens help in low light. Ear thermometers offer quick readings but require technique; forehead non-contact options minimize contact spread.
