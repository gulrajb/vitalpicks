Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are these devices?

Most consumer-grade devices are 95–99% accurate under normal conditions. Medical-grade versions are FDA-cleared and hospital-validated.

Do they require a prescription?

Most don't. However, some continuous glucose monitors require a prescription. Check your specific device.

How often should I use it?

Daily for best results. Consistent tracking reveals patterns and helps you optimize health habits.

Are they covered by insurance?

Some are, especially if medically necessary. Check with your insurer before purchasing.

Final Verdict

The right health device transforms how you understand your body. Start with what matters most to you—heart health, glucose, or body composition—and track consistently. Small daily data compounds into big health wins.
