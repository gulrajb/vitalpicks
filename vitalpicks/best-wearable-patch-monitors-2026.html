Frequently Asked Questions

Are wearable patch monitors accurate?

Yes, FDA-cleared devices like KardiaMobile and Oura Ring have been validated in clinical studies for detecting arrhythmias and sleep patterns.

Can I wear a patch monitor while sleeping?

Most patches are designed for 24/7 wear and are particularly useful for tracking sleep quality and detecting nocturnal heart rate anomalies.

Do patch monitors work with my phone?

All modern devices sync via Bluetooth to iOS and Android apps for real-time alerts and historical trend analysis.

Is my data private?

Reputable manufacturers use end-to-end encryption and comply with HIPAA standards to protect sensitive health data.
