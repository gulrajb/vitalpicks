Frequently Asked Questions

Do lifting straps help with grip strength?

Straps reduce grip fatigue but don't build grip strength—use them for heavy pulls only.

What's the difference between cotton and synthetic straps?

Synthetic straps are more durable; cotton straps are more comfortable and breathable.

Can beginners use lifting straps?

Yes, but focus on grip strength first before relying heavily on straps.
