🔬 The Science of Creatine

Creatine monohydrate increases your muscles' phosphocreatine stores, allowing you to produce more ATP (energy) during high-intensity exercise. The result: more reps, heavier lifts, faster recovery. After protein, it's the most evidence-backed supplement you can take.

All you need is plain creatine monohydrate. Ignore "creatine HCL", "buffered creatine", and other expensive variants — they offer no proven advantage over plain monohydrate.

💡 How to Take Creatine

Loading or not?

You can either load (20g/day for 5 days) to saturate muscles quickly, or just take 5g/day and reach the same saturation in 4 weeks. Loading works faster but isn't necessary.

When to take it

Timing doesn't matter much. Take it whenever is convenient — before or after your workout, or with breakfast. Consistency matters more than timing.

Do you need to cycle?

No. Studies show it's safe for continuous long-term use. You don't need to take breaks.
