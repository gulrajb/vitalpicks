Why Magnesium Matters for Recovery

Magnesium is a critical mineral for muscle relaxation, sleep quality, and post-workout recovery. While magnesium deficiency affects up to 50% of people, supplementation can significantly improve sleep onset, reduce muscle soreness, and enhance overall recovery.

Types of Magnesium

Different forms have different benefits: Magnesium glycinate for absorption, magnesium citrate for digestive support, magnesium threonate for cognitive function, and magnesium malate for energy.

Dosage & Timing

Effective doses range from 200-400mg daily. For sleep, take 30-60 minutes before bed. For muscle recovery, split doses throughout the day. Always consult a healthcare provider.
