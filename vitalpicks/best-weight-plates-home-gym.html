FAQ

Q: What's the difference between bumper plates and standard plates?
Bumper plates are made of rubber and are designed for dropping from overhead, while standard plates are metal and better for regular lifting. Bumper plates are more durable for CrossFit and Olympic lifting.
Q: How do I know what weight to buy?
Start with a total of 100-150 lbs for beginners. You can always add more plates later as you get stronger. Consider a mix of different weights for versatility.
Q: Are expensive products always better?
Not necessarily. Mid-range products often offer the best value. Don't overpay for brand names alone — focus on durability and functionality.
