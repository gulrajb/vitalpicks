Frequently Asked Questions

Is liquid chalk better than powder chalk?

Liquid chalk is cleaner and longer-lasting; powder chalk is cheaper and more traditional.

Can I use baby powder instead of gym chalk?

No—gym chalk has magnesium carbonate for grip; baby powder won't provide the same hold.

Is chalk banned in gyms?

Many gyms restrict loose chalk; liquid chalk is usually allowed as it produces less dust.
