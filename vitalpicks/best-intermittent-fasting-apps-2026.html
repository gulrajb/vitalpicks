Frequently Asked Questions

Is intermittent fasting safe for everyone?

Intermittent fasting is generally safe for most adults, but pregnant women, those with a history of eating disorders, and people taking certain medications should consult a doctor first.

How long does it take to see weight loss results?

Most people notice weight loss within 2-4 weeks of consistent intermittent fasting, though results vary based on diet quality and exercise.

Can I drink water or coffee while fasting?

Yes! Water, black coffee, and tea without added calories are all allowed during fasting windows. They help manage hunger.

What's the best fasting protocol for beginners?

The 16:8 protocol (16 hours fasting, 8-hour eating window) is most beginner-friendly. Start with 12:12 if needed and gradually extend.
