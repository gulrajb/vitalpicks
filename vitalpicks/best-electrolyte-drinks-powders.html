Buying Guide

Look for electrolytes with optimal sodium-potassium ratios (1:1 or similar). Choose based on taste preference, price, and convenience (powder vs. tablets vs. ready-to-drink). Consider your activity level and climate.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I drink electrolytes?

During or after intense exercise lasting 60+ minutes, or in hot/humid conditions.

Are electrolyte drinks better than water?

For intense workouts, yes. For light activity, water is sufficient.

Can I make my own electrolyte drink?

Yes, mix water, salt, sugar, and lemon juice. Commercial options offer convenience and tested ratios.

Do electrolytes help with hydration?

Yes, sodium and potassium enhance water absorption in the gut.
