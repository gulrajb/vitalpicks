FAQ

What size medicine ball should I start with?

Beginners typically start with 6-10 lbs. This allows proper form and control while building core strength. As you progress, move to 14-20 lbs.

Can medicine balls replace dumbbells?

They complement each other. Medicine balls are better for explosive, functional movements. Dumbbells are better for isolated strength work.

How often should I use medicine balls?

2-3 times per week is ideal. They're intense on joints, so adequate recovery is important.

Are wall balls better than regular medicine balls?

Wall balls (with flatter surface) are better for throwing against walls. Regular medicine balls are more versatile for general training.
