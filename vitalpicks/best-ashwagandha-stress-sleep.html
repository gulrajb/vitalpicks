Buying Guide

Look for standardized extracts (KSM-66 or Sensoril). Typical dose is 300-600mg daily. Choose between capsules and powder based on preference. Take consistently for 4-8 weeks for best results.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does ashwagandha take to work?

Most people notice benefits after 2-4 weeks of consistent use.

Can I take ashwagandha with other supplements?

Generally safe, but consult your doctor if taking medications for anxiety or sleep.

Is ashwagandha safe for long-term use?

Yes, it's well-tolerated. Some recommend cycling off periodically.

What's the best time to take ashwagandha?

Morning or evening works. Evening is better if using for sleep.
