FAQ

Do push-up handles really make a difference?

Yes! They allow 2-3 inches deeper range of motion, increase chest activation, and reduce wrist strain significantly.

Can I do all push-up variations with handles?

Most variations yes. Avoid explosive plyometric push-ups on cheap handles — stick to controlled movements.

How far apart should the handles be?

About shoulder-width (12-18 inches apart). Wider = more chest, narrower = more triceps.

Are rotating handles worth the extra cost?

For casual home training, no. For serious athletes, they reduce shoulder stress.
