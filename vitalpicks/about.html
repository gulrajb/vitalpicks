About VitalPicks

We exist to cut through the noise in the health and wellness market — so you never waste money on products that don't work.

Our Story

VitalPicks was started after one too many disappointing purchases — overpriced supplements with inflated claims, fitness gadgets that broke in a month, and review sites that were clearly just paid advertisements dressed up as honest opinions.

We decided to do something different. Every product we recommend is researched thoroughly, cross-referenced with customer reviews, and evaluated against clinical evidence where available. We cover both the Indian and US markets because great health products shouldn't be limited by geography.

How We Make Money

VitalPicks participates in the Amazon Associates Program and other affiliate programs. When you click a link on our site and make a purchase, we earn a small commission — at absolutely no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the site running and all reviews free.

Our editorial opinions are never influenced by affiliate relationships. We recommend products we genuinely believe in, and we'll tell you when something isn't worth buying even if we could earn a commission on it.
