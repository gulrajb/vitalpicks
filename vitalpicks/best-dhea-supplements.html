Buying Guide

Start with 25-50mg daily and increase gradually. Men typically need 50-100mg, women 25-50mg. Take in the morning with food. Cycle DHEA: 5 days on, 2 days off, or 6 weeks on, 1-2 weeks off to maintain receptor sensitivity. Monitor for acne, mood changes, or hair growth — signs dosage is too high.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is DHEA safe to supplement?
Yes, at recommended doses (25-100mg). Long-term safety at megadoses is less studied. Follow cycling protocols.
Q: What does DHEA do?
Precursor to testosterone and estrogen. Supports muscle growth, fat loss, bone density, mood, energy, and libido.
Q: How much DHEA should I take?
Start 25mg daily, increase to 50-100mg. Women generally use lower doses. Never exceed 200mg daily.
Q: Best time to take DHEA?
Morning with breakfast for best absorption. DHEA follows a circadian rhythm, peaking in the morning.
Q: Can women take DHEA?
Yes, but at lower doses (25-50mg). Monitor for facial hair growth, acne, or voice deepening. Cycle aggressively.
