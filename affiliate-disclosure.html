Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: March 2026

Summary (Plain English)

Some links on VitalPicks.org are affiliate links — meaning if you click them and buy something, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the site free. It never affects what we recommend.

Amazon Associates Program

VitalPicks.org is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com, Amazon.co.uk, Amazon.ca, and other Amazon affiliate sites.

As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases. This means when you click an Amazon link on our site and make a purchase, we receive a small commission (typically 1%–10% depending on product category) at absolutely no additional cost to you.

Other Affiliate Programs

In addition to Amazon Associates, VitalPicks.org may participate in other affiliate programs including but not limited to:

  • ShareASale
  • Commission Junction (CJ Affiliate)
  • Direct brand affiliate programs

Any time we use an affiliate link, there will be no indication that changes the price or availability you see — the only difference is we may earn a commission.

How This Affects Our Reviews

It doesn't. Our editorial process is completely independent from our affiliate relationships. Here's our firm policy:

  • We never accept payment from brands to rank products higher
  • We never adjust our scores or recommendations based on commission rates
  • We review and recommend products we genuinely believe are worth buying
  • When a product isn't worth buying, we say so clearly — even if we could earn a commission recommending it
  • We include negative reviews and honest cons in every product writeup

FTC Compliance

This disclosure is provided in compliance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on the use of endorsements and testimonials in advertising (16 C.F.R. Part 255). The FTC requires websites to disclose any material connections between reviewers and the products they promote.
